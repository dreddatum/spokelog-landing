// How It Works

Three steps.
Zero paperwork.

Built for superintendents and GCs who don't have time to sit at a desk.

01 / RECORD

Speak Your Log

Tap record on your phone. Talk about the day — subs, materials, equipment, any issues. Natural language, no script needed. Even works in a noisy truck cab.

02 / EXTRACT

AI Extracts Everything

Spokelog uses Whisper + Claude AI to pull out labor hours, material deliveries, equipment status, safety incidents, and RFI flags. Formatted to CSI MasterFormat standards.

03 / EXPORT

Review & Export PDF

Review the extracted data, make any edits, then export a professional, branded PDF daily log — ready for your owner, PM, or legal file. In 60 seconds flat.

// Features

Built for the
job site.

Every feature designed around how GCs actually work — not how software engineers think they work.

Voice First

Works on any phone, any browser. Tap once to record. No app to install. Uses OpenAI Whisper for accurate transcription — even with construction slang and background noise.

Smart Extraction

AI automatically identifies and structures labor tracking, material deliveries, equipment status, safety incidents, and RFI flags from your natural speech.

Professional PDFs

Branded, letter-format daily log reports with your company name. Includes weather data, labor summary, delivery log, safety status, and signature lines. Print or email ready.

OSHA & CSI Compliant

Output follows CSI MasterFormat division codes, OSHA 300 log standards, and ASTM E2691 guidelines. Defensible documentation that holds up on claims.

Auto Weather Data

Weather conditions are automatically pulled from OpenWeather API for your project location. Temperature, wind, precipitation — all stamped on every log without typing a word.

Secure & Private

Your voice recordings and log data are encrypted at rest and in transit. Audio deleted from processing pipeline within 24 hours. You own your data — always.
