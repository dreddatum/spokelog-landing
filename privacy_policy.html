Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Effective: January 1, 2025

Spokelog is built for construction professionals. We collect only what we need to provide our service, we never sell your data, and you can delete everything at any time.

1. Who We Are

Spokelog ("we," "our," or "us") is a software-as-a-service platform that helps General Contractors and field superintendents create AI-powered daily construction logs. Our website is spokelog.com. For questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@spokelog.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information — name, email address, company name
  • Voice recordings you submit for transcription
  • Project details and construction log data you enter or review
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card numbers)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location (city/region level only)
  • Usage data — which features you use and when
  • Error logs to help us fix bugs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To transcribe your voice recordings and generate construction logs using AI
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Spokelog service
  • To send you service-related emails (account notifications, receipts)
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not use your voice recordings or log content to train AI models without your explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate Spokelog (cloud hosting, payment processing, AI transcription).
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest). Voice recordings are deleted from our transcription pipeline within 24 hours of processing.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — update inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability — export your construction logs in PDF or JSON format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Spokelog is a professional tool intended for users 18 years of age or older.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you by email at least 14 days before material changes take effect.
